Significance: Hi-C has been used to study how the 3D genome organization of drosophila responds to heat stress (Molecular cell 2015 and PNAS 2019). However H3K27ac and H3K27me3 are mixed in Hi-C, here we apply in situ ChIA-PET to enrich H3K27ac and H3K27me3 separately to better explore how active and inactive regions are functionally changed in 3D chromatin architecture in response to heat stress.
